The Bootcamp Framework

4 Weeks from Idea to Production

A progressive, hands-on curriculum. Each week builds on the last — so by week 4 you have a real, deployed product running on Google Cloud.

1

Discover

Foundation week. Explore AI Studio, test starter apps, understand Gemini’s capabilities — text, vision, code, and multimodal.

2
